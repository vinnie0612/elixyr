elixyr image upload



upload from cli

just post your image to the /upload endpoint

example:
curl -X POST -F "file=@image.jpg" https://elixyr.tld/upload
example_id

you can now access your uploaded image at https://elixyr.tld/i/example_id

accepted file extensions are:
'png', 'jpg', 'jpeg', 'gif', 'bmp'
max file size 10MB
